Salon Etiquette

1. Arrive on Time

We value your time and ours. Arriving on time ensures you get the full experience of your service and helps us stay on schedule for all clients.

2. Cancel or Reschedule in Advance

Life happens — we get it! If you need to cancel or move your appointment, please give us at least 24 hours’ notice so we can adjust accordingly.

3. Silence Your Phone

To maintain a relaxing environment, we kindly ask that you silence your phone during your appointment. Feel free to use it quietly if needed, but please avoid speakerphone or loud conversations.

4. Respect Others’ Space

Our salon is a shared space. Please be mindful of others who are enjoying their treatments and respect personal space and volume levels.

5. Communicate Clearly

Be open about your preferences, expectations, or any allergies or sensitivities. The more we know, the better we can serve you!

6. No Extra Guests (Unless Necessary)

To keep the salon calm and comfortable, we kindly ask that you come alone unless you need assistance or have a scheduled group appointment.

7. Tipping is Appreciated

Tips are not required, but always appreciated for a job well done! They go directly to your stylist or technician.

8. Cleanliness & Hygiene

Please arrive with clean hair, nails, or skin as appropriate for your service. Our team upholds strict hygiene standards to keep everyone safe and comfortable.

9. Children in the Salon

We love little ones! But for safety and relaxation reasons, children should only accompany you if they have an appointment or if arrangements are discussed in advance.

10. Enjoy Your Experience

Most importantly, relax and enjoy your time with us! Our team is here to pamper, style, and make you feel your absolute best.
