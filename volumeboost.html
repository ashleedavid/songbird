Volume Boost Mousse

Available In-Store Only
Size: 150ml

Product Overview

Our Volume Boost Mousse is a lightweight, airy styling foam that delivers long-lasting lift and fullness without stiffness. Formulated with pro-vitamin B5 and heat-activated polymers, it adds body and structure to fine or limp hair while keeping it soft, touchable, and bouncy.

Why We Use It

  • Adds instant volume and lift at the roots
  • Lightweight and non-sticky formula
  • Enhances natural texture and shape
  • Ideal for blowouts, updos, or everyday styling

How It’s Used in the Salon

We apply it to damp hair, focusing on the roots, before blow-drying to create volume and movement. It's also great for setting rollers or adding grip to fine strands before styling.

Note

This product is available exclusively in our physical salon. If you’re interested, feel free to ask about it during your next visit.
