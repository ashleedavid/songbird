Detangling Leave-In Mist

Available In-Store Only
Size: 200ml
₱295

Product Overview

The Detangling Leave-In Mist is a lightweight, hydrating spray that makes brushing and styling effortless. Enriched with aloe vera, argan oil, and vitamin B5, this mist softens strands, reduces breakage, and smooths frizz — making it perfect for daily use on all hair types.

Why We Use It

  • Instantly detangles knots and snarls
  • Adds moisture and shine without buildup
  • Controls frizz and flyaways
  • Safe for color-treated and chemically processed hair

How It’s Used in the Salon

We spray it on clean, towel-dried hair before combing or styling. It's especially helpful for clients with dry, curly, or thick hair, and works great as a prep before blow-drying.

Note

This product is available exclusively in our physical salon. If you’re interested, feel free to ask about it during your next visit.
