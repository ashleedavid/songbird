Silky Repair Shampoo is a salon-favorite, crafted to restore and strengthen dry, brittle, and chemically-treated hair. With a blend of keratin and argan oil, this shampoo gently cleanses while nourishing each strand from root to tip — leaving hair softer, shinier, and visibly healthier.
