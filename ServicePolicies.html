Service Policies

Appointments

We highly recommend booking your appointment in advance to secure your preferred time and stylist. Walk-ins are welcome, but availability is not guaranteed.

Late Arrivals

If you arrive more than 10 minutes late, your appointment may need to be rescheduled or shortened out of respect for the next client. Please call us if you're running late so we can do our best to accommodate you.

Cancellations & No-Shows

Cancellations must be made at least 24 hours in advance. No-shows or repeated last-minute cancellations may result in a booking restriction or a cancellation fee on future appointments.

Payments

We accept cash, major credit/debit cards, and selected mobile payments. Prices are subject to change without prior notice. Please confirm rates during booking if unsure.

Satisfaction Guarantee

If you’re not satisfied with your service, please let us know within 48 hours. We’re happy to offer touch-ups or adjustments when appropriate. Refunds are not offered for completed services.

Product Usage

We only use salon-grade, tested, and trusted products to ensure the best results. If you have allergies or sensitivities, please inform us ahead of time.

Guests & Children

To ensure a relaxing atmosphere, we kindly ask that only those receiving services be present in service areas. Children must be supervised at all times.

Respect for Staff & Clients

We maintain a safe, respectful environment for everyone. Harassment or inappropriate behavior toward staff or other guests will not be tolerated and may result in refusal of service.
