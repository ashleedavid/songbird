Heat Protection Spray

Available In-Store Only
Size: 200ml
₱330

Product Overview

Our Heat Protection Spray is formulated to guard hair from the damaging effects of heat styling tools like blow dryers, flat irons, and curling wands. Infused with vitamins, antioxidants, and a blend of natural oils, this spray provides a protective barrier, leaving your hair smooth, shiny, and healthier with every use.

Why We Use It

  • Protects hair from heat damage up to 450°F (232°C)
  • Adds moisture, shine, and smoothness
  • Prevents frizz and helps retain hair’s natural texture
  • Ideal for all hair types and can be used daily

How It’s Used in the Salon

We apply the spray to damp hair before blow-drying or using any heat styling tools. It helps reduce heat damage while providing a sleek, frizz-free finish.

Note

This product is available exclusively in our physical salon. If you’re interested, feel free to ask about it during your next visit.
